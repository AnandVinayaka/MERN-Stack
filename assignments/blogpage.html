Ingredients:

Soil

Uses: It is the main ingredient used in brick making process. Best quality of soil is chosen to create a strong and perfect brick that can remain unaffected for moisture and sunlight for years.

Rice husk

Uses: Husk that comes from rice is game changer in this brick making process. It is mixed in the brick making mixture that will make brick lighter and stronger and helps the brick to burn and bake from inside.

Fly Ash

Uses: Fly Ash is used to make brick lighter and stronger. Using this product will make a brick more fire-resistant due to their non-porous nature and high temperature resistance.

Coal

Uses: Coal for brick baking is bituminous coal. It is preferred for its high carbon content, which provides the heat needed to bake the bricks, and its moderate ash content, which helps to maintain a consistent heat during the baking process.
